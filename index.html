About Centrinian

Independent Publishing House

Founded in London on August 5, 2011, Centrinian publishes a broad range of quality fiction and non-fiction titles for adults and children.

Our Mission

Fresh ideas for a new generation of readers

Centrinian is a London-based independent publisher bringing passion for storytelling to readers everywhere. The company specialises in creative and innovative stories filled with mystery, magic and history, and welcomes compelling voices with a fresh vision.

Centrinian’s three divisions include Non-Fiction, Fiction, and Children’s Books. Its aim is to bridge the gap between fresh ideas and a new generation of readers while pursuing quality over quantity.
